Manners Cost Nothing

A Gentle Reminder About Professional Etiquette

Because good manners make everyone feel valued and respected

Dear Professional Contact,

Thank you for reaching out. I appreciate your interest in opportunities at my company.

However, I must respectfully point out that requesting referrals from people you don't know personally is not considered good professional etiquette.

Here's why this matters:

  • A referral is a personal endorsement based on knowledge of your work and character
  • Asking strangers for referrals puts them in an uncomfortable position
  • It can damage your professional reputation
  • Most importantly, it shows a lack of respect for the other person's time and position
